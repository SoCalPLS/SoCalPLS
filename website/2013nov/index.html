SoCal Fall 2013: Programming Languages and Systems

Reminder: Don't forget to register!

The SoCal Programming Languages and Systems Workshop is an informal gathering of programming languages and systems researchers in Southern California. We meet next at UCLA on Saturday November 23, 2013.

Goals

The primary goals of this workshop include
  • Providing a friendly environment for students (and other researchers) to present their work
  • Strengthening ties between local institutions
  • Learning about cool stuff happening in PL and systems!

Important Dates

November 6 Abstract deadline (end of day)
November 13 Notification of acceptance
November 15 Registration deadline
November 23 SoCal workshop

Submission Guidelines

Both original research and work that has been published in other venues is welcome. Authors should submit abstracts of at most one page (~300 words) to todd|@|cs|.|ucla|.|edu by November 6, 2013. Selected abstracts will be presented as a talk or a poster.
