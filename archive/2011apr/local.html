SoCal Spring '11: Programming Languages and Systems

The SoCal Programing Languages and Systems Workshop is an informal gathering of programming languages and systems researchers in the southern California region.

SoCal will next meet at Harvey Mudd College on Saturday, April 16, 2011.

The primary goals of this workshop include

  • Providing a friendly environment for students (and other researchers) to present their work
  • Strengthening ties between local institutions
  • Learning about cool stuff happening in PL and Systems

This SoCal meeting will consist of contributed talks and posters.
