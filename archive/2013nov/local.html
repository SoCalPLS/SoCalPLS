SoCal Fall 2013: Programming Languages and Systems

Location: Room 2444 in Boelter Hall at UCLA.

Parking: Please park in Lot 9 (circled on the map below). If you have a parking permit from one of the UC campuses, it is valid at all UCs. If you do not have a UC parking permit, there is a kiosk (circled on the map, and labelled "i" for information) just next to the entrance to Lot 9 on Westwood Blvd., where you can purchase an all-day permit.

Directions: The following map shows the parking lot and Boelter Hall (each circled in red). Once you park, follow the red arrow on the map, enter the door at the southeast corner of Boelter Hall. Walk on the Lo and Behold! tiles up a small staircase, and Boelter 2444 is immediately on the right. Interactive UCLA maps are available here.
